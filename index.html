Welcome!

This platform aims to serve as a learning hub for concepts in experimental fluid mechanics research that can sometimes be tricky to grasp and are not widely available and some open-source engineering tools that make advanced experiments and analysis accessible. Here, you will find interactive tutorials on optical flow diagnostic techniques, open platforms designed to make experimental fluid dynamics more precise and accessible and an overview of my investigations into physics of turbulent flows.

Flow Visualization of a submerged jet

Flow Visualization of a submerged jet in a 15L glass tank using fluorescent dye (Rhodamine B) illuminated with a 532nm laser. A 560nm long pass optical filter is used to eliminate scattered signals and isolate the fluorescence signal.
